More Info

As we know that upward migration occurs due to the cohesion and ashesion of molecules within the snowpack, and we also know that the concentration in harmful halogen ions in within the arctic snowpack is dependent on these same ions present in the sea water, we can therefore conclude that we must shift our focus from the concentration of ions present within the arctic snowpack to the concentration of these same ions within the sea water itself.
Since the high concentrations of ions within the snowpack come from the concentration of ions within the seawater, it is very important to look into whether these high concentrations of dangeroud halogen ions are coming from human created high concentrations of ions in the seawater relative to any natural processes that lead to these high concentrations of dangerous halogen ions in the seawater.
